About Mobius


With a little more than 100,000 residents, Mobius is the most populous city in the county and the perfect place to start exploring everything the Coast offers. The compact downtown is a half-mile squared, served by a Caltrain station, with restaurants, cafes, and a range of shops. South of downtown is Central Park, that’s home to the Japanese Garden featuring pplantations, tea house, koi pond, and bamboo grove, .The park is also home to a rose garden, a mini train, and the Mobius Arboretum There is multiple beaches and historical sites in the city as well.
