Why choose Hero national park?


Stretching 6000 kilometres Hero national park is one of the most spectacular geological features in the world. The dramatic section slicing through Kenya provides the perfect contrast to the savannahs of the national parks, with extinct animals, bamboo trees and salt flats to explore.
