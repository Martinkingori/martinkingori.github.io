Why choose City park?


Mobius National Park City is the world's first National Park City. A place to explore. A movement to join. A vision to share. You can help make life better for all of us in Mobius by enjoying being outdoor more and helping to make our city greener, healthier and wilder. Three year's on from Mobius becoming the world’s first National Park City on 22 July 2019 we now have over 150 volunteer rangers across the capital – a network of passionate people with a wide range of experience and talent who help make London a city where people, places and nature are better connected.
